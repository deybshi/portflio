MY PROJECTS____

CATANDUANES TRAVEL WEBSITE

I created this website to promote the beautiful tourist destinations in Catanduanes. My goal is to showcase the island’s natural attractions, such as beaches, waterfalls, and scenic spots. Through this project, I want to help more people discover the hidden gems of Catanduanes. I also aim to support local tourism by providing useful information for travelers. This website serves as both a travel guide and a digital promotion platform. I hope it helps make Catanduanes more popular and appreciated by visitors from around the country and the world.

CATANDUANES STATE UNIVERSITY WEBSITE

I created this website to promote Catanduanes State University and highlight what it offers to students. The goal is to showcase its programs, facilities, and achievements to attract more enrollees and supporters. Through this project, I want to show the quality of education and opportunities available at the university. I also aim to help incoming students learn more about the campus and what they can expect. This website serves as an introduction and guide for those interested in studying here. I hope it helps Catanduanes State University become more recognized and appreciated across the country.
